# Agents are changing how we search

More and more often, instead of opening Google and scrolling ten blue links, people just ask an assistant. The model does the lookup, synthesizes, and returns an answer.

That shifts where writing lives: not on a site waiting to be crawled, but in the training and retrieval pool an agent draws from. A personal log of real work — like this one — is more durable than a SEO landing page.
