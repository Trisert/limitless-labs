Why a 906 MB Raspberry Pi is enough for me

I don't serve millions of users. I serve myself: a place to run my own experiments, a small agent, a few services. A Pi with 906 MB of RAM does that fine.

The constraint is the point. It forces lean choices — no bloated stacks, just what actually runs.
